Design Change Drivers (Architect-Driven)

9 items — net +R 1,561,352

1. Marmoran "Permasuede" Finish

+R 182,000

NEW item. 192m² external walls @ R750/m² + 40m² narrow widths @ R950/m². Replaces standard paint on these elevations. Licensed Marmoran applicator, 15-year international guarantee. Colour: "Sushi Wrap".

2. Wall Tiling Upgrade

+R 72,300

PC tile rate doubled (R350 → R750/m²). Area increased 102 → 132m². Architect upgraded external wall tiling specification.

3. Steel Staircase + Cage

+R 250,000

Staircase R100K → R250K (+R150K). Fence & gate around staircase R50K → R150K (+R100K). Per Drg 800 (metalwork).

4. Aluminium Trimmers & Flashings

+R 84,350

ALL NEW: sill plates 48m, F-T Capco drips 45m, slab edge sill 38m, header capping 84m, parapet capping 26m — 241m total at R350/m. Per Drg 901 / 902.

5. Waterproofing Extension

+R 203,143

Derbigum doubled (190 → 436m²), rate R550 → R650/m². New: reveals & thresholds 275m @ R200/m = R55,000. +139.9%.

6. Balustrading & Handrails

+R 105,000

Balustrades R120K → R200K. New handrail PS R25,000. Per Drg 603.

7. Post-Tensioning

+R 297,500

Area doubled 402 → 827m² @ R700/m² (R281K → R579K). Moved from Bill 2 to Bill 12 provisional sums.

8. Polished Concrete Floors

+R 157,200

Area increased 634 → 1,027m² @ R400/m² (R254K → R411K). Moved from Bill 2 to Bill 12.

9. Roof Construction

+R 231,190

R350K → R1,100K gross; consolidates roof steelwork previously in Bill 7 (R532,810). Net +R231,190 after reclass.
